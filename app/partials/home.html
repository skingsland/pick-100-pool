March Madness Pick 13 Tournament Pool

An exciting twist on the typical March Madness Pool, where you only pick 13 teams.

  • Tired of the same ol' March Madness pool every year?
  • Sick of picking every single game, when all you really care about is a handful of teams?
  • Wish you had a slick online bracket, instead of emailing MS Excel files back and forth?

Well then, you need to play the Pick 13 March Madness pool this year! It's easy; you just pick 13 teams, whose seeds add up to 100. That's it!

The rules for the game are simple: Everyone picks 13 teams whose seeds add up to 100. For example, if UNC is a # 1 seed and VCU is a #13 seed and you pick them, they combine to make 14, and you need to select 11 other teams whose seeds add up to 86.

You earn points everytime one of your chosen teams wins a game. If a team wins a game, you get the value of their seed plus a designated point amount, which ascends each round as follows: 1, 2, 4, 8, 16, 32. So, if you picked VCU as a #13 seed and they win games in the first three rounds, you would get 13+1, 13+2, and 13+4.
